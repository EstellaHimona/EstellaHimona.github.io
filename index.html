Bootcamp 2020: Illusion scene

So now it's time to show you my first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2020). I created an illusion scene in which circles are fading in and out of the screen, in a certain way so to create something like a maze of circles. As you will watch the project running, you will find out that the movement of the cirlces synchronises with the background music I added to the sketch! While running this project, it is highly recommended to keep your eyes focused on the white circle which appears in the centre of your screen!!
